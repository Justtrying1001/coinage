# COINAGE — Roadmap & Build Plan (Updated MVP MICRO)

## Product source of truth (April 17, 2026)

- **MVP MICRO now includes all standard non-special buildings**.
- **MVP MICRO scope is city-level gameplay only**.
- **Premium / special / deferred buildings remain out** (`training_grounds`, `shard_vault`, wonders/unique/endgame branches).
- **No wallet, no holding validation, no premium unlocks** in this pass.
- **MACRO gameplay is deferred to pass 2** (world projection, cross-city warfare, colonization, token-world effects, inter-faction governance).

## MVP MICRO content (playable now)

1. Economy & logistics: `hq`, `mine`, `quarry`, `refinery`, `warehouse`, `housing_complex`, `market`.
2. Military: `barracks`, `combat_forge`, `space_dock`, `military_academy`, `armament_factory`.
3. Defense: `defensive_wall`, `watch_tower`.
4. Research: `research_lab` + local city research points + instant unlock projects.
5. Intelligence: `intelligence_center` + local intel readiness/projects.
6. Governance: `council_chamber` + local policy/edict choices.

## Phase plan

### Pass 1 — MVP MICRO (current)
- Full city loop with all standard buildings.
- Construction queue + troop training queue.
- Local derived stats for production/training/defense/intel/research/logistics.
- Persistence for economy + military + research + intel + local governance state.
- `city3d` delivered as a premium command-deck UX (top bar + branch rail + immersive city stage + adaptive context panel).

### Pass 2 — MACRO systems (next)
- World map projection and travel resolution.
- Cross-city warfare lifecycle and territorial effects.
- Colonization and sector control outcomes.
- Inter-faction governance and token-world hooks.

### Pass 3 — Premium/special (later)
- Special buildings and premium unlock paths.
- Wallet/holding integrations only after macro stability.
