# Coinage — Understanding Snapshot (Post-MVP MICRO Re-scope)

## Current product truth

- The playable MVP is **MICRO city gameplay**.
- MICRO includes **all standard non-special buildings** (17 total).
- Premium and special/deferred branches remain excluded.
- Wallet/holding/premium unlock paths are disabled in this pass.

## Gameplay layer split

### MICRO (implemented now)
- City resource economy.
- Construction and progression across all standard branches.
- Troop training, local military bonuses.
- Local defense, research, intelligence, governance, market/logistics effects.

### MACRO (deferred next pass)
- World projection and cross-city warfare.
- Colonization and territorial outcome simulation.
- Inter-faction governance and token-world interactions.

## Why this matters

The repo no longer treats military/defense/research/intelligence/governance as “later” for MVP.
Those are now part of the immediate playable city loop, while macro systems remain staged for a dedicated follow-up pass.
